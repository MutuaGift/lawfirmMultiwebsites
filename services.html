Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our legal services and processes.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling us at +254 721 157 732, emailing info@jmkathiliadvocates.co.ke, or using the contact form on our website. We offer both in-person and virtual consultations to accommodate your preferences.

What are your legal fees and billing structure?

Our fees vary depending on the complexity of your case. We offer various billing arrangements including:

Hourly rates for ongoing legal matters
Fixed fees for specific services
Retainer arrangements for regular legal support
Contingency fees for certain litigation matters

During your initial consultation, we'll provide a clear estimate and discuss the most suitable billing arrangement for your needs.

How long does a typical legal matter take to resolve?

The duration varies significantly based on the type and complexity of the matter:

Simple documentation: 1-2 weeks
Contract negotiations: 2-4 weeks
Property transactions: 4-8 weeks
Litigation matters: 6 months to 2+ years

We provide regular updates and realistic timelines throughout your case.

Do you offer emergency legal services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency legal services for urgent matters such as arrests, injunctions, and time-sensitive legal issues. Call our emergency line at +254 721 157 732 for immediate assistance.
