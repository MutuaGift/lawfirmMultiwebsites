1+ Year of Legal Excellence

Founded in May 2025, J.M. Kathili & Company Advocates has established itself as a premier law firm in Kenya. Our firm was built on the principle that exceptional legal service should be accessible, personalized, and results-driven.

Over the years, we have grown from a small practice to a respected firm with three dedicated legal experts, each specializing in different areas of law to provide comprehensive legal solutions to our clients.

Our track record of successfully handling over 50 cases speaks to our commitment, expertise, and the trust our clients place in us. We take pride in our ability to navigate complex legal challenges and deliver favorable outcomes.

Meet Our Founding Partner

Adv. James M. Kathili

Managing Partner / Founder

With over a decade of experience in civil litigation and commercial law, Advocate Kathili brings strategic insight and formidable advocacy skills to every case. He holds an LLB from the University of Nairobi and was admitted to the Kenyan Bar in 2014.

His philosophy is simple: provide exceptional legal counsel that not only solves immediate challenges but also positions our clients for long-term success. With offices in Machakos, Nairobi, and Matuu, we're strategically positioned to serve clients across different regions while maintaining our commitment to excellence and personalized client service.
